👥 Team Sync
How team sharing works
  1. Put sprint-dashboard.html + team-sprint-data.json in a OneDrive folder shared with your team
  2. Each tester opens the dashboard, clicks "Load from team file" → picks the shared JSON
  3. Make your changes (edit, add, delete sprints)
